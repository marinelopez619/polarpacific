The Team

Michael Proctor

I am a business partner in Polar Pacific Properties and LMK Enterprises, LLC. I have been a real estate investor since June 2015. I reside in Southern California and have two grown children, and two grandchildren. I began my working career at age 11. I worked in my grandfather's cabinet shop where he taught me many valuable lessons and a good work ethic. I continued working for him through high school, while also working in retail sales. I learned the importance of good customer service, effective communication and interpersonal skills. After high school, I moved on to the printing business, where quality and timeliness were keys to success. Looking for a new challenge, and a better quality of life, I began working with my father in his construction company. I started from the bottom and worked up to a supervisor position in a few short years. We built and remodelled houses, worked on commercial projects and completed state contracts for Cal Trans. I then obtained my own contractor's license and started my own company. I successfully ran my company for about five years, until the needs of my growing family required a more consistent work schedule and benefits. I attended the Law Enforcement training academy and became a law enforcement professional. For the past 27 years I have served my community with honor and good ethics, taking to heart the oath I swore. I have also maintained my General Contractor’s License and have acted as GC on our local projects. The gradual growth of our business will soon enable me to retire from law enforcement and work on RE investing full time. I am excited to work toward achieving our goals and pursuing our passions.

Lisa Rosas

I was born and raised in Northern California, and moved to San Diego County in 2012. I have three children, and six grandchildren. My passions are cooking/baking, outdoor adventures, and service projects which help military servicemembers and people in need of a hand-up. At an early age, I learned the value of setting goals, working hard and giving back. I have worked in retail sales, customer service, marketing/ advertising, grant writing and education. I also assisted in the start-up of two HVAC/ Sheet Metal Fabrication companies, then owned/operated a small candy-making company before deciding on a career in education. I was an educator for 18 years. I spent 10 years as a teacher at Christian elementary schools, 2 years an administrator, and 6 years working with special needs and At Risk students. In June 2014, I received a traumatic brain injury in the classroom. I was unable to return to teaching and needed to pursue new career opportunities. I decided on Real Estate, since I had some experience in Real Estate investments. I enlisted the help of Michael Proctor, who became my business partner, and we established our Real Estate Investment company in 2015. We had each owned investment properties in the past and had performed renovations. We attended Real Estate Investment training together and began “flipping” properties. I then obtained my CA Real Estate Agent license. We have flipped properties in six states and now own SFRs and Multi-Family properties in 2 states. We are constantly researching various demographics and market indicators to locate the best markets for investment. Our current efforts are focused on locating, vetting, and acquiring more multi-family investments, through partnerships or syndication.
