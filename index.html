About Us

Our goal is to use Real Estate Investing as a vehicle to fund our passion for serving others. Our mission is to impact lives in a positive way by providing opportunities, resources, and tools for their success. We have flipped properties in six states and now own SFRs and Multi-Family properties in 2 states. We are constantly researching various demographics and market indicators to locate the best markets for investment. Our current efforts are focused on locating, vetting, and acquiring more multi-family investments, through partnerships or syndication.
